\# Baseline Evaluation



The Linear Regression model achieved:



MAE  = 0.206 kg



RMSE = 0.253 kg



R²   = 0.870



The MAE value indicates that predictions

are off by approximately 0.206 kg on average.



The RMSE value is low and close to the MAE,

indicating that large prediction errors are rare.



The R² score of 0.870 indicates that

approximately 87% of the variation in crop

yield is explained by the model.



Since R² is greater than 0.80, the model

provides an excellent baseline for future

comparison with Random Forest Regression.
