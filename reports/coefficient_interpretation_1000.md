\# Linear Regression Coefficient Interpretation



The Linear Regression model was trained using

temperature, humidity, and CO₂ concentration.



Temperature coefficient = 2.8370



This positive coefficient indicates that

higher temperature is associated with

higher crop yield.



Humidity coefficient = 1.7381



This positive coefficient indicates that

higher humidity is associated with

higher crop yield.



CO₂ coefficient = -2.3961



This negative coefficient indicates that

higher CO₂ concentration is associated

with lower predicted yield in this dataset.



Overall, temperature and humidity showed

positive influence on crop yield while

CO₂ showed a negative relationship.
